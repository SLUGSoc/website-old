SLUGSoc LAN Events

We've run over 70 LAN events here at SLUGSoc. They're normally hosted at the Ridge building on Ranmoor student village, giving our players free rein to set up their gear and adventure together across an assortment of games. LAN favourites include Quiplash 2, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, Overwatch and Guns of Icarus.

What's a LAN?

A LAN party is a gathering of people with computers or compatible game consoles, where a local area network (LAN) connection is established...for the purpose of playing multiplayer video games together.

Wikipedia
If you've never been to a LAN before, look forward to a fun weekend of gaming! Bring your console/desktop PC and get ready to enjoy your favourite games with friends. That aside, we provide cheap snacks, lightning-fast internet and a sleeping area.
Games aren't all, though - you can expect a great playlist, a quiz, and the undying LAN tradition of Pizza Time. Perhaps we'll screen a film too?
