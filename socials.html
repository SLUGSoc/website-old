About Socials

SLUGSoc meets weekly for socials on Tuesdays. We normally host these at Huttons Bar, where we drink, chat and enjoy fun multiplayer games! You can see our socials schedule for the next few weeks here - click on each one to learn more.
Over the holidays, we might even run unofficial socials - you'll see those listed here too. If you're just getting started with SLUGS, join us at socials each week to get involved!
